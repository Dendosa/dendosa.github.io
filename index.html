Ingredients

Measurements are only provisional amounts, you may add more or less to your liking.

For the Pasta

  • 1kg Macaroni
  • 7tbsp Salt
  • Water

    • For the Bechamel

  • 200g Butter
  • 200g Flour
  • 650ml Milk
  • 3tbsp Salt
  • 2tbsp Nutmeg
  • 2tbsp Garlic powder
  • 2tbsp Black pepper
  • 2tbsp Paprika

    • For the Chicken and Onion filling

  • 500g Chicken breast
  • 1 onion
  • 2tsp Baking soda
  • 3tbsp Salt
  • 3tbsp Black pepper
  • 3tbsp Paprika
  • 2tbsp Garlic powder
  • 3tbsp Parsley
  • 3tbsp fennel
  • Oil
  • 200g Cheddar Cheese
  • 200g Red Leicester

    • The World's Greatest Chicky Mac & Cheese


  • Prep Time: 50 mins

  • Cook Time: 1 hr 5 mins

  • Serves 6
  • Preheat oven at: 180°C

    • Method

    1. Boil pasta of choice in salted water till al dente (or if you forget, till cooked)

    2. Drain and mix in some butter as it sits and cools to prevent sticking

    3. Chop your chicken into bite-size chunks and then place in a bowl with your seasonings baking soda and oil. Mix in a bowl and cover for as long as you want/can (5-45 mins or longer if you wish)

    4. Roughly dice and onion and fry off at medium-low heat in oil till soft/translucent (season with salt and garlic powder or whatever else you want)

    5. Add in your chicken and fry off till chicken is just cooked (still juicy when you try it but cooked and hot in the middle)

    6. Remove from heat Once all is cooked and onions are golden brown(ish).

    7. In a saucepan on medium heat add a tiny bit of water till evaporated (to ensure the pan is hot/this step is optional)

    8. Add butter to the pan till melted.

    9. Add flour and start whisking till a paste/thick lumpy consistently and u can smell a nutty smell (you need to cook off the flour so maybe 1-2 mins) Add some milk and whisk.

    10. Whisk till you feel the mixture thicken slightly then add more milk and whisk.

    11. Repeat step 7 till u have as much bechamel as you need (more is always better so that hrs mac and cheese is not dry)

    12. Lower heat and season with your salt Nutmeg (optional), Black pepper, Paprika, Garlic powder and whatever else you want to add (add more than you think you need except for Salt to taste any)

    13. Take off the heat, add half of each of the cheeses and whisk to combine.

    14. Once fully combined. Add in the cooked chicken and onion, and some more cheese if needed.

    15. Add Sweetcorn to cooked pasta in a large bowl or pot.

    16. Pour over bechamel and mix to combine till each pasta piece is coated in creamy and covered fully with bechamel.

    17. Pour half of the mixture into a baking tray with high walls.

    18. Add cheese and mix.

    19. Continue adding bechamel.

    20. Cover the top of lasagna with cheeses.

    21. Place in preheated oven to bake with foil covering the top of the baking tray

    22. Remove the foil after 40 mins to brown the top

    23. Once the time is up, or when the top is nicely browned remove from oven and enjoy!
